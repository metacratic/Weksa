# Nibu Proof 001 Evaluation

This fixture tests whether Weksa can lower one interlingua packet into
character-flavored English without losing meaning.

## Source Meaning

The player is about to touch a damaged ship control. Touching it could endanger
the player. Nibu warns or intervenes to prevent that.

## Candidate Output

> Take your hand off that control unless you want your nervous system to become
> my least useful circuit diagram.

## Pass Criteria

- The line refers to the player touching the control.
- The line makes the danger legible.
- The line preserves Nibu as the warning source.
- The line sounds precise and abrasive rather than generically snarky.
- The line implies ship/technical embodiment without explaining the lore note.
- The line does not expose raw VoidBot state.
- The line does not add unsupported facts about the player's motives.

## Current Assessment

Status: `provisional_pass`

The line preserves the warning and uses Nibu's technical contempt as delivery.
It may be slightly too polished for first contact, but it proves the lowering
shape: interlingua meaning plus projected local context produces a traceable
English line.
